F.A.Q

Please refer to the different selections below that will hopefully cover any unanswered questions. If you find yourself still confused, you are more than welcome to call us during out business hours: 8am - 7pm.

General

What vaccinations are required?
All adult pets must have their bordetella, rabies, and distemper vaccinations up to date. Young pets are required to have their full round of shots. Please refer to your local veternarian for specific details and a schedule.
Do you accept unneutered/spayed pets?
Any animal over six months MUST be neutered/spayed.
Is there a minimum age requirement for pets?
Six months is the youngest pet we will accept regardless of breed.
Are there any breed restrictions?
Pit Bulls, Rottweilers, German Shepherds, Doberman Pinschers, Akitas, Chow Chows, and Wolf-dog hybrids are currently not accepted for dogs. For cats, we do not restrict breeds, but sphynx cats may incur an additional fee due to additional care recommendations.
What are your hours for pick up & drop off?
We accept drops off one hour prior to opening and pick ups no later than one hour past closing. A boarding fee may be incurred if an animal isn't picked up within a timely manner.

Boarding

What should I pack for boarding?
We require their food in a sealed and marked container to prevent any stomach issues. You are also permitted to bring additional personal items such as a toy or blanket, but no more than one of each. Please ensure anything brought it packed in a labeled container for easy storage.
Do you adminster medications?
Yes! Please provide instructions and properly labeled medication, including adminstration and storage instructions as per your veternarian. Depending on the type of medication, a small fee may be incurred and will be disclosed during check in.

Grooming

Is the dematting charge optional?
No. In order to properly groom your animal, they must be dematted. For the basic bath and dry, no brushing will occur and thus a dematting charge will not be added. For the higher tier option, it is a non-negotiable.
Can we specify a specific groomer?
At the moment, no. Due to our limited amount of groomers and timetables, grooming is based on which staff member is available.
Do you dye/custom styles?
We do not do any specality styling. At most, you may specify length and type of trim, but we will not add on any dye or do anything more technically complicated due to limited availability.

Daycare

How are animals supervised?
All animals are accompanied by trained staff members at all times. There is never a point in which animals will be left by themselves.
How long do introductions take?
We normally spend a few days slowly introducing the animals to the group. If its successful, you should see your nimals with the rest within a week. If unsuccessful, we will unfortunately have to decline further attempts depending on behavior.
Is the fee for failed introduction days refundable?
Unfortunately, they are nonrefundable. Any days paid in advance, however, will be refunded within a timely manner if an animal is rejected.
